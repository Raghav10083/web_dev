ABOUT WOBT
Every enduring institution begins with an idea.
White Owls & Black Tigers (WOBT) was founded on a simple belief: the world's greatest opportunities and most complex challenges can no longer be solved by a single profession. They require the collective intelligence of experts from diverse disciplines working together with a shared purpose.
WOBT is a global multidisciplinary professional ecosystem that brings together expertise across law, finance, technology, business, governance, research, academia, innovation, and public policy. Through its specialised platforms, WOBT enables professionals to collaborate, exchange knowledge, develop strategic relationships, create innovative solutions, and contribute to the advancement of industries and society.
Rather than being defined by any one profession, WOBT is defined by excellence, integrity, innovation, and collaboration.
The White Owl
The White Owl derives its inspiration from the vahana of Goddess Lakshmi, symbolising prosperity, abundance, and good fortune in Indian tradition. Across the world, the owl has long represented wisdom, intelligence, knowledge, foresight, and the ability to see opportunities beyond the obvious.
Within WOBT, the White Owl represents intellectual capital.
It symbolises researchers, innovators, entrepreneurs, technologists, scientists, financial experts, strategists, educators, consultants, investors, business leaders, and every professional whose knowledge creates value, advances ideas, and drives progress.
The White Owl represents curiosity, learning, innovation, and the continuous pursuit of excellence.
The Black Tiger
The Black Tiger represents courage, leadership, discipline, resilience, responsibility, and decisive execution.
Its black colour reflects professionalism, integrity, and the commitment to uphold justice, ethics, governance, and accountability. While inspired by the black attire traditionally associated with the legal profession, the Black Tiger represents a much broader philosophy — protecting institutions, managing risk, resolving disputes, enforcing standards, and leading with conviction.
Across the WOBT ecosystem, this spirit is reflected in advocates, arbitrators, compliance professionals, corporate leaders, governance specialists, investigators, regulators, risk advisors, public servants, and professionals entrusted with protecting rights, organisations, and society.
Our Philosophy
Knowledge creates opportunity. Courage transforms opportunity into achievement. The White Owl represents the ability to imagine, innovate, analyse, and create. The Black Tiger represents the ability to execute, protect, lead, and inspire confidence. Together, they define the philosophy of White Owls & Black Tigers.
OUR ECOSYSTEM
WOBT is an umbrella platform that develops and operates specialised initiatives serving professionals, businesses, institutions, governments, investors, and society.
Its ecosystem includes platforms dedicated to professional networking, legal technology, litigation funding, dispute resolution, education, research, knowledge publishing, artificial intelligence, business solutions, strategic advisory, and future innovations that advance professional excellence across disciplines.
Each platform operates independently while remaining united by WOBT's shared values of integrity, collaboration, innovation, and excellence.

WOBT exists so that a strong matter is never lost for want of money, expertise, evidence, technology, or reach. In practice, that means:

WOBT manages and tracks every matter in its network — hearings, filings, deadlines, and case status — in one place, so nothing is missed and the client always has visibility. (WOBT LexManage)
4. SOFTWARE & AI FOR ADVOCATES AND LAW FIRMS

WOBT guides, trains, updates, and equips the advocates and law firms in its network — from practice-management support to skill-building for the next generation of lawyers. (WOBT PartnerGrow, WOBT Young Minds, WOBT CareerNext)
6. SECURE EVIDENCE STORAGE — AUTHENTICITY PRESERVED
Evidence and case documents are stored through WOBT's technology with their authenticity and chain of custody intact from submission to production in court. (WOBT LawBox)
7. FORENSIC AUTHENTICATION LAB
A dedicated forensic lab verifies the authenticity of signatures, photographs, videos, and other digital or physical evidence — so a document's genuineness is settled before it becomes a courtroom fight, not during one.
8. GLOBAL NETWORK FOR CROSS-BORDER DISPUTES
Where a dispute crosses borders, WOBT connects Indian advocates and law firms with vetted counsel abroad and coordinates strategy across jurisdictions. (WOBT LexPartners)
9. PARALEGAL SUPPORT
Paralegal services — research, drafting, filing, and case preparation — extend a law firm's capacity for a matter without extending its headcount.
10. GLOBAL DOCUMENT PROCUREMENT
Need a record, document, or piece of evidence held in another jurisdiction? WOBT's network sources it through the right local channel, wherever in the world it sits.

WHAT IS MINIMUM INVESTMENT?
The minimum investment threshold varies depending on the type of matter and funding structure. Typically, WOBT considers matters with a minimum claim value that ensures viable returns post-litigation costs. Our team evaluates each case individually to determine appropriate funding thresholds aligned with risk and recovery potential.
HOW DO WE CALCULATE THE VALUATION?
Funding is provided on a non-recourse basis unless otherwise agreed in writing — if the funded matter is unsuccessful, the funded party owes no repayment to WOBT. In the event of a successful outcome, WOBT is entitled to an agreed percentage or structured return from the recovery proceeds, as set out in the specific funding agreement. Funding decisions, amounts, and terms are determined case by case and are not standardised across matters. This is confirmed language from your Disclaimer/Policy document (clause 2.3); it deliberately doesn't fix a percentage or formula, since none is standardised.
